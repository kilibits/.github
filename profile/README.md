## Our Vision

Technology should serve the people who use it. Too often, solutions built elsewhere don't fit the realities of life in Tanzaniaâ€”our infrastructure, our languages, our ways of doing business, our communities. We're changing that.
- We build tools that work offline in areas with limited connectivity.
- We design interfaces in Swahili first.
- We create payment systems that integrate with mobile money like M-Pesa/Airtel Money etc.
- We build for mobile first
- We solve problems that matter in our context.

The goal is to inspire creators to *rethink and reimagine* and shape what solutions should look like.
